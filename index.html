Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our photo editor

Is Photo Editor Pro really free?

Yes, our basic photo editing features are completely free to use. We offer premium upgrades for advanced features, but the auto-enhancement and background replacement are available at no cost.

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG, WebP, and HEIC. Downloaded images are provided in high-quality JPG format by default.

How does the background replacement work?

Our AI automatically detects the main subject in your photo, removes the existing background, and replaces it with a contextually appropriate background based on your image content.

Are my photos stored on your servers?

We take privacy seriously. Your photos are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers. They are automatically deleted after processing or within 24 hours.

Can I use this for professional work?

Absolutely! Many professional photographers and content creators use our tool for quick edits and background replacements. The output quality is suitable for both web and print media.
